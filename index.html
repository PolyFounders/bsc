About the Startup Challenge

The Bronco Startup Challenge is an annual pitch competition that gives students the opportunity to pitch their proposals to a panel of judges.

Every year, we receive hundreds of submissions but only the best will be in the running to win cash prizes. The total prize pool for this year is $10,000!

Students who participate in the competition will also meet and network with local business leaders and venture capitalists.

The Bronco Startup Challenge will be held on the 12th of May from 5pm - 9pm
